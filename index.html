About Us

Welcome to Shakespeare and Company, a legendary bookstore nestled in the heart of Paris, France. Established in 1919 by Sylvia Beach, the original Shakespeare and Company was a haven for expatriate writers of the "Lost Generation," including Ernest Hemingway, F. Scott Fitzgerald, and James Joyce.

Today, our bookstore carries on Sylvia Beach's legacy as a sanctuary for literature lovers from around the world. We specialize in English-language books, ranging from classics to contemporary works, and we take pride in our curated selection that reflects the diverse voices and stories of the literary world.

Step inside our cozy, book-filled rooms, where every nook and cranny holds the promise of discovery. Whether you're seeking a beloved classic, a hidden gem, or the latest bestseller, our knowledgeable and passionate staff are here to guide you on your literary journey.
